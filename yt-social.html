YouTube Social



Direct message sharing and co-watching experience within YouTube.





Design Lead | Jan 2015 - Feb 2017

Problem



For the past decade, YouTube has been the leader of providing video content to the world. Yet to share a YouTube video, users must rely on 3rd parties (Facebook, etc). As social giants Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat and Whatsapp started to grow their video content in 2015, Youtube's lack of social experiences became a huge competitive disadvantage.

Solution



Our team's goal was to make YouTube the best place to share and chat about video with friends. We did this by first creating a direct sharing feature within YouTube, then incorporating a co-watching experience within it, allowing users to watch videos and converse at the same time.

My Role & Process



When I started on this team, it had churned for a year without a design strategy. I helped the team switch to an iterative design and research process (Lean UX), which let us innovate faster. As the Design Lead, I produced designs, planned OKR goals, mentored interns, and managed the deliverables of contractors and visual designers.

  • Foundational Research


    I helped organize and attended several in-home user sessions led by a freelance UX Researcher.

  • Design Sprint &
    Defining Key Opportunities


    Participated in 2 week long design sprint, and led the creation of concept videos to illustrate our product direction.

  • Information Architecture


    Worked with PM & Eng to map out user flows for both the sharing and video recieving experience.

  • Design Iterations using the Lean UX methodology


    Quick user research with concept mocks to validate the product direction.

  • Prototypes, High fidelity mocks, illustrations & motion design


    Created prototypes using framer.js. Design specs were created in sketch and aftereffects.

  • Marketing video


    Provided art direction on our marketing video, to be released with the launch announcement.

  • Additional Features


    Continued to test and ideate on new features post-launch.

Impact



This product was designed and shipped within two months. Since launch, it has increased lead generation for Opendoor's main product, and reduced the cost for each lead by almost 50%. Due to very positive responses from users, iteration and expansion is a top company priority for 2017.
